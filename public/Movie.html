The Best Movies According to Ellis

My Top 5 Movies of all time.

The Lord of the Rings

This is Tolkiens classic work interpreted into Peter Jacksons movie.

The Hobbit

The prequel to Lord of the Rings, a visual feast.

Forest Gump

A tale of innocence, which question are perception of intelligence.

Schindlers List

A harrowing Holocaust tale that glimpses the darkness of the human soul that thrives today.

Going Postal

Terry pratchett's book interpreted as a movie, with all of his comedic adroit perspective of human behaviour.
